What is DRIFT?

Drift is a ride sharing service that allows people to rent electric scooters for a short period of time.

How to Drift?

  1. Download the Drift app
  2. View nearby scooter on the app
  3. Scan the QR code or enter number to unlock
  4. End ride in the app and lock up safely when finished

The drift scooter

Just SRD 1 to start your ride.
